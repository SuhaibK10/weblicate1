# Weblicate — Brand Redefinition

**From web agency to technology consultancy.**
This document is the single source of truth for the new positioning, copy, architecture, and design system.

---

## 1. Positioning Statement

> Weblicate is a technology consultancy that designs and engineers the software organizations run on — digital platforms, AI systems, and industry infrastructure for businesses that intend to lead.

One line, internal use:
**"We don't build websites. We build the systems behind the business."**

---

## 2. Brand Story

Weblicate started in November 2024 with a simple observation: most organizations run on software that was never built for them. Spreadsheets doing the work of systems. Off-the-shelf tools bent out of shape. Processes held together by habit and effort.

The founder, an ML engineer, had already shipped fraud detection pipelines, e-commerce platforms, and research systems — and kept seeing the same thing everywhere: the gap between what a business does and what its software understands.

Weblicate exists to close that gap. Not with templates. Not with retainers that never end. With software designed around how an organization actually operates — and engineered to be depended on.

A website is one output of that mission. It was never the mission.

## 8. Typography

| Role | Recommendation | Free fallback |
|---|---|---|
| Display + UI | Söhne / Suisse Int'l / Neue Haas Grotesk | **Geist** or Inter |
| Editorial accent (pullquotes only) | Tiempos | **Newsreader** or Instrument Serif |
| Labels, indices, data | — | **Geist Mono** or JetBrains Mono |

- H1: `clamp(3.5rem, 9vw, 8rem)`, leading 0.95, tracking −0.02em
- Section headings: `clamp(2.5rem, 5vw, 4.5rem)`
- Body: 1.0625rem / 1.7
- Labels: 0.6875rem mono, uppercase, tracked +0.12em, numbered `(01)`–`(08)`

The numbered mono section labels already exist on the site — keep them. They are the most consultancy-grade element currently there.

## 9. Color

| Token | Value | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `bg` | `#0A0A0A` | page |
| `surface` | `#141414` | panels |
| `fg` | `#F7F6F3` | warm off-white text (warmer = editorial) |
| `muted` | `#A8A6A0` | secondary text |
| `rule` | `rgba(247,246,243,0.12)` | hairlines |
| `accent` | `#4D6BFE` | interaction only — links, focus, one CTA |

Rule: the accent appears on **interactive elements only**, never decoration. Everything else is monochrome. Remove the multicolor team-card gradients and rotating hero word colors — polychrome reads "agency."

## 10. Animation

- Section entrances: 12px fade-rise, 0.4s, staggered 60ms. Nothing else moves on scroll.
- H1: line-by-line mask reveal on load (once).
- Hairline dividers draw in from left as sections enter.
- Numbers count up (already built — keep `Counter`).
- Hover: images shift from muted to full, 0.5s. Links get accent underline draw.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` globally.
- Delete: marquees, glow effects, gradient rings. Motion budget: if two things animate at once, cut one.

## 11. Design System Notes

- Spacing scale: 4 / 8 / 12 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 48 / 64 / 96 / 160. Section padding: 160px desktop, 96px mobile.
- Max width 1200px, 12-col grid, generous gutters.
- Prefer **rows and hairlines over cards**. Cards say marketing site; ruled rows say index of capabilities.
- Corners: 4px max where surfaces are unavoidable. No pills except tiny status badges.
- Buttons: primary = solid off-white on black; secondary = 1px hairline. Two variants only.

## 13. CTA Strategy

- **One primary action sitewide:** Start a conversation. Every section funnels to it.
- No pricing tables on the homepage. Consultancies scope; agencies price. (Keep pricing for the FAQ or a quiet /pricing page if lead qualification needs it.)
- Reduce friction with certainty, not discounts: "20 minutes is enough" + "proposal within 48 hours."
- Secondary CTAs are navigational only (See the work, Read the philosophy).
